About Me

Hello! I'm Jahanvi, a creative and intuitive individual with a passion for design and technology. My journey has been an exciting blend of art and science, allowing me to explore various fields and sharpen my skills across different platforms.

Professional Background

With 4 years of experience as a graphic designer and UI/UX designer, I have had the opportunity to work on diverse projects, ranging from website redesigns to custom web development. My professional journey has honed my skills in creating visually appealing and user-friendly designs that meet client expectations and enhance user experiences.

Education

I have a strong academic background in design and digital media. I recently graduated in Web Development and UI/UX Design from Red River College in Winnipeg, Canada. My studies have provided me with a solid foundation in both creative and technical aspects, enabling me to bring innovative ideas to life.

Hobbies

In my free time, I enjoy expressing myself through painting, listening to music, and creating intricate henna art. These hobbies not only relax me but also inspire my work, infusing creativity and energy into my projects.

Additional Skills

In addition to my design and web development expertise, I have gained knowledge in video editing and 3D software, including Maya. These skills allow me to add depth and dimension to my projects, bringing them to life in unique and engaging ways.
