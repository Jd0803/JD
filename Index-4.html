Poster Maker

HTML, CSS, JS
Web Service
2024
The Custom Truck Art Poster Maker project is an innovative and creative web Service inspired by the vibrant and intricate truck art commonly seen in South Asia. This project allows users to design personalized posters by adding custom text, selecting various artistic elements, and saving their creations as PDFs or images. The primary aim was to create a user-friendly platform that celebrates the cultural heritage of truck art while providing users with a fun and engaging design experience.
The development of the Custom Truck Art Poster Maker involved several key stages. Initially, extensive research was conducted on traditional South Asian truck art to understand its unique elements, color schemes, and motifs. This research informed the design phase, where I created a range of customizable templates and artistic components for users to choose from. The technical development phase included building the web application using modern web technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Process

To enhance the user experience, I incorporated features like drag-and-drop functionality, a live preview of the poster, and options to adjust text size, font, and color. Additionally, I implemented a save feature that allows users to download their finished posters as high-quality PDFs or images. Throughout the development process, user feedback was actively sought and incorporated to refine the application's functionality and design.

The Custom Truck Art Poster Maker project was a resounding success, resulting in a visually appealing and highly functional web application. Users appreciated the intuitive interface and the ability to create unique, culturally rich posters with ease. The project not only highlighted the beauty of South Asian truck art but also provided a platform for users to express their creativity. Future plans for the application include adding more customization options, such as different themes inspired by various cultural art forms.
